Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Tech Week Wizard (“Wizard”, “we”, “our”, or “us”), a tool built to help you find Tech Week events and explore relevant fashion options.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email Address: When you voluntarily provide your email address for session tracking and personalization
  • Session Data: Unique session identifiers to track your usage patterns
  • We do not collect sensitive information (e.g., financial data, health data, or government-issued IDs)

Usage Information

  • Search Queries: The event search terms you enter
  • Interactions: Button clicks, filter selections, and navigation patterns
  • Preferences: Gender selection for outfit recommendations
  • Event Interactions: Which events you view, outfit recommendations you request

Technical Information

  • Browser Information: Browser type, version, and device information
  • IP Address: For basic analytics and security purposes
  • Cookies: Session cookies to maintain your preferences

How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: To provide event recommendations and outfit suggestions
  • Personalization: To improve recommendations based on your preferences
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and improve our service
  • Communication: To send you relevant updates about Tech Week SF events (if you provide email)

Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your data is stored securely and is not shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

Data Retention

We retain your session data and interactions for analytics purposes for up to 12 months. Email addresses are retained until you request deletion or opt out of communications.

Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from communications at any time

Cookies and Tracking

We use session cookies to maintain your preferences and track interactions for analytics purposes. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect functionality.

Third-Party Services

If you click on an external link (e.g., event websites, brand stores), you will be redirected to third-party pages. We are not responsible for how those sites handle your data.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
